At CynviaOne, we are committed to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have regarding your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

When you create or manage an account with CynviaOne, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal details: such as your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Account activity: including last sign-in date, account creation date, and login methods.
  • Security details: such as your chosen password (securely encrypted).
  • Device information: details about the device(s) used to access your account.
  • Notifications preferences: if you choose to allow updates and alerts.

We only collect information necessary for providing and improving our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your CynviaOne account.
  • Provide secure login and account recovery options.
  • Send you important account notifications (e.g., password changes, login alerts).
  • Improve security and protect against unauthorized access.
  • Provide personalized features and services if you choose to enable them.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your data is stored securely on trusted servers with encryption and strict security measures. We regularly monitor our systems to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or loss of data.

We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information only in the following cases:

  • With your consent: if you choose to connect your account with other apps or services.
  • For legal reasons: if required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or government requests.
  • For security reasons: to prevent fraud, protect the safety of users, and safeguard CynviaOne services.

5. Your Privacy Controls

You are always in control of your data. You can:

  • Update your personal details in Personal Info
  • Manage security settings in Security & Sign-in
  • View and manage devices in Your Devices
  • Manage your account anytime at CynviaOne Account
